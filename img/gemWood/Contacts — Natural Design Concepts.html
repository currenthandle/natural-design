Dana Pettem creator and founder of Natural Design Concepts has taken a modern swing at natural functional art. Using Gem Stones and quality Jungle Woods mixed with special resins and LED light technology, she has created a collection of luxury tables, mirrors and sculptures, that will catch the eye of any design enthusiast.

Natural Design Concepts is a dedicated team that is looking forward to fulfilling your requests. Please contact us anytime and we will be happy to answer your questions.
